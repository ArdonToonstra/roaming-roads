Our Essence: The Compass

This is the core of 'roaming roads'. Our mission, vision, and values form the compass for every journey we share and every decision we make. They define who we are and why we do this: to inspire authentic travel.

Our Core Values

Adventurous

We encourage exploration and stepping off the beaten path. We seek the unique and unexpected.

Independent

100% ad-free and affiliate-free. Our content is unbiased and driven by genuine experience.

Honest

We provide a real, unfiltered look at travel - the good, the bad, and the unexpected.

Our Voice: The Travel Companion

Our tone is a balance of practical and playful. We are the knowledgeable, down-to-earth friend you ask for real travel advice. Our voice is short, powerful, and lively, reflecting the joy of travel.

Our Look: The Horizon

Our visual identity evokes a feeling of energy, adventure, and discovery. It's professional, modern, and inspiring, with a clean aesthetic that lets our photography shine.

Logo

Usage Guidelines

  • The circular form represents the globe and a complete journey.
  • Colors evoke sunrise and sunset over diverse landscapes.
  • Maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'r' in 'roaming'.
  • Don't: stretch, distort, re-color, or alter the logo in any way.

Typography

Headings: Poppins Bold

Used for all titles and major headlines to create impact and grab attention.

Body: Lato Regular

This is the Lato font, chosen for its clarity and readability. It ensures a comfortable reading experience, even for longer travel stories. The focus is on clarity.

Photography Style

Authentic, high-quality photography is the heart of our brand. It must feel real and immersive.

  • Content: Dynamic, real moments. We capture vibrant landscapes, interesting details, and genuine travel experiences. Our photos should never look like generic stock images.
  • Editing Style: Photos should be edited to be bright, crisp, and colorful. The style should be consistent (e.g., using the same preset), enhancing natural beauty without looking fake. Think vibrant colors, good contrast, and a clean finish.

In Action: The Destination

Here you can see how all the elements of the brand guide come together in practice. These are the blueprints for our main content formats, designed for an immersive and user-friendly experience.

Homepage Header

  • Visual: A full-width, breathtaking photo of a scenic road or landscape.
  • Layout: Logo centered at the top.
  • Headline: A powerful, central headline in Poppins Bold: FIND YOUR NEXT ROAD.
  • Sub-headline: A clear mission statement in Lato: "Authentic travel guides, honestly written. No ads. No affiliate links. Ever."
  • CTA: A prominent button in Sunset Orange with the text "EXPLORE GUIDES".

Travel Story / Blog Post

  • Interactive Map: A central element of the page. The map displays the entire route with numbered pins for each step or major point of interest.
  • Numbered Steps: A list of steps corresponds to the pins on the map. Clicking a step (e.g., "Day 1: Arrival & Exploration") scrolls to that section and highlights the pin on the map.
  • Header: The post title in Poppins Bold over a full-width hero image from the trip.
  • Step-by-Step Content: The body text is broken down by the numbered steps. Each section contains the story, photos, and tips related to that part of the journey.
  • Structure: Subheadings in Poppins Bold are used within each step's content for clarity.
  • Practical Tips: A "Trip Notes" box with a Warm Sand background can be used within each step for specific advice (e.g., restaurant recommendation, ticket info).
